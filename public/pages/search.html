Responsibilities

ByteDance is a global incubator of platforms at the cutting edge of commerce, content, entertainment, and enterprise services - over 2.5 billion people interact with ByteDance products, including TikTok.

Why Join Us

Creation is the core of TikTok's purpose. Our platform is built to help imaginations thrive. This is doubly true of the teams that make TikTok possible.

Together, we inspire creativity and bring joy - a mission we all believe in and aim towards achieving every day.

To us, every challenge, no matter how difficult, is an opportunity; to learn, to innovate, and to grow as one team. Status quo? Never. Courage? Always.

At TikTok, we create together and grow together. That's how we drive impact - for ourselves, our company, and the communities we serve.

Join us.

About the Team

Join our dynamic marketing team based in São Paulo, Brazil, where creativity meets strategic thinking. Our team is composed of passionate professionals dedicated to driving brand awareness, generating leads, and creating impactful marketing campaigns.

We foster a collaborative environment that values innovation, diversity, and the unique perspectives each team member brings.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute comprehensive marketing strategies to promote our products and services in the Brazilian market.
  • Work with Regional and Global Media and Marketing leads to creation and implementation of multi-channel marketing campaigns, including digital, social media, print, and events.
  • Analyze market trends and competitor activities to identify opportunities for growth and improvement.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including sales, product development, and customer service, to ensure alignment and support for marketing initiatives.
  • Manage the marketing budget, ensuring effective allocation of resources and ROI.
  • Monitor and report on the performance of marketing campaigns, using data to optimize and refine strategies.
  • Build and maintain relationships with media, influencers, and key stakeholders to enhance brand visibility.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry developments and emerging marketing trends to keep our strategies fresh and competitive.

Qualifications

Minimum Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, Communications, or a related field.
  • Marketing experience, preferably within a similar industry.
